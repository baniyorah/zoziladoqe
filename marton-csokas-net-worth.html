Marton Csokas Wiki Biography

Marton Paul Csokas was born on 30 June 1966, in Invercargill, New Zealand, of Hungarian descent through his father who was a mechanical engineer, and English, Danish, and Irish through his mother who was a nurse. Marton is known for appearing in various hit films such as “The Lord of the Rings” film series, “Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter”, and “The Equalizer”. All of his efforts have helped put his net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Marton Csokas? As of mid-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $4 million, mostly earned through a successful career in acting. Other films he’s been a part of include “xXx”, “Kingdom of Heaven” and “Aeon Flux”. He’s also had various television projects, and as he continues his career, it is expected that his wealth will also continue to increase.

Marton Csokas Net Worth $4 million

Marton started his career in the New Zealand drama “Shark in the Park” in which he played Terry Mercer. In 1993 he became a part of the soap opera “Shortland Street”, and stayed with the show for two years, then had a role in the film “Broken English” in 1996, before being cast in a recurring role in “Xena: Warrior Princess”, which continued until 2001. He then portrayed Celeborn, in “The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring” and would reprise his role in “The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King”. In 2005, he was cast in “Kingdom of Heaven” which he played Guy de Lusignan. He also had the villain role in “xXx” and was part of “The Bourne Supremacy”. He was also cast in “Aeon Flux”. His net worth started to increase with all these opportunities opening up for him.

Csokas then ventured to stage productions, appearing in “Who’s Afraid of Virginia Woolf?” and “Peribanez”. Moving back to film, in 2007 he was cast in “Romulus, My Father” which won him an AACTA Award and a Film Critics Circle of Australia Award, followed by a role in Tim Burton’s “Alice in Wonderland” before becoming a part of the comedy “With Love… from the Age of Reason”. In 2011, Csokas played in the thriller “Dream House”, before getting cast in “Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter. He then got an AACTA Award nomination for his work in “Dead Europe”, and in 2013 he co-starred with Michael Chiklis in the thriller “Pawn” before starring in “The Amazing Spider-Man 2”. He also became a part of the “Sin City” sequel, “Sin City: A Dame to Kill For”.

The following year, he was cast in “The Equalizer” and one of his latest projects is the AMC series “Into the Badlands”. He also appeared with Emilia Clarke in “Voice from the Stone” which was released in 2017. He’s also working on “Mark Felt: The Man Who Brought Down the White House”.

For his personal life, Marton’s relationship, if any, is kept private. Csokas holds citizenship in both New Zealand and Hungary.
