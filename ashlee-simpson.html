Marital Life with second husband, Evan Ross

Ashlee is currently married to an American actor and musician, Evan Ross. The two tied the knot on 31 August 2014 at Ross' mother estate in Connecticut. Their marriage ceremony was held privately with few guests, their close friends, and relatives.

Ashlee and Evan started dating in July 2013, and after a year, the duo got engaged in January 2014. Back in the days when they were still dating, the couple appeared together at the Los Angeles Premiere of "The Hunger Games: Catching Fire" at Nokia Theatre L.A where some of the well-known personalities such as Sam Claflin and Jennifer Lawrence were present.

"Happy Anniversary, my love, it has been the best three years of my life. I love knowing that I get to wake up next to you for the rest of my life. Thanks for giving me the best love."
