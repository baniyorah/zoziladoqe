Who is Key Glock?

Key Glock is an American Rapper, and songwriter, who rose to fame in 2017 as a Young Dolph protege with his album Glock Season.

He is signed to Young Dolph’s record label Paper Route Empire.

Key Glock- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Glock was born on August 3, 1997, in South Memphis, Tennessee.

As of 2023, his age is 26 years old. In terms of nationality, he holds an American identity. Markeyvius LaShun Cathey is his birth name.

He was raised by his grandmother and aunt. His mother was sent to prison when he was 20 months old where she would stay for the next 15 years. During his youth, LaShun and his grandmother visited his mother at the prison.

Key Glock- Professional Life, Careers

In 2017, Young Dolph signed Key Glock to his Paper Route Empire label.

He released his first single Racks Today with American Rapper Jay Fizzle on January 25, 2017.

Whereas, his first mixtape Glock season was released on June 16, 2017.

Key released Yellow Tape on January 31, which reached number 14 on Billboard 200. Son of a Gun, his sixth mixtape was released on May 22, 2020.

Key and Young Dolph released the single ‘Aspen’ on March 5, 2021, and it was reported the release of their second album, Dum & Drummer 2, on March 26, 2021.

Later, Key released the song “Proud” a tribute to Young Dolph on January 18, 2022.
