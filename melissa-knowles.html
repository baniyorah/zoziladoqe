Melissa Knowles Biography

Melissa Knowles is an American Journalist and Correspondent who was born and brought-up in Houston, Texas. Currently, she works at HLN-TV in Atlanta, Georgia as an Anchor and Correspondent for ‘Morning Express’ with Robin Meade, ‘The Daily Share’ and ‘Weekend Express’. Prior to joining the HLN news team, she worked at Yahoo!, as a Host of “Trending Now,” The Summer Olympic Games in London, and Super Bowl XLVII.

Melissa began her journalism career at Channel One News as an anchor and at Fox Sports as an anchor and sideline reporter covering the Detroit Pistons. She also worked for BET News, as a correspondent, covering the presidential inauguration in 2013. She served as an anchor for “Big 12 This Week” on ESPNU, a weekly college sports show.
