Before discussing Len Goodman Wife, let’s discuss Len Goodman’s professional career and his cause of death. 

Len Goodman was an English professional ballroom dancer along with a dance judge and coach.

He appeared on the UK Television program Strictly Come Dancing as head judge where various celebrities compete for the glitter ball trophy. He ran a ballroom dance school in Dartford, Kent.

He was born on 25 April 1944 as Leonard Gordon Goodman in London, England, and passed away on 22 April 2023 at the age of 78 in Royal Tunbridge Wells, Kent, England from bone cancer in a hospice.

He was diagnosed with prostate cancer in March 2009. He was treated surgically at a London Hosptial. He underwent surgery in 2020 for a small facial melanoma.
