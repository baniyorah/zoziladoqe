Paul Walkers Daughter Meadow Shares Rare Cheeky Pics of Her Fianc Louis Thornton-Allan

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Patria Henriques

"Fast and Furious" actor Paul Walker’s daughter Meadow, looks happy and in love after sharing a sweet picture of her fiance, actor Louis Thornton-Allan on her Instagram. Here’s some detail about their love story.

When Paul Walker died in a tragic car crash some years ago, he left behind millions of fans and colleagues to mourn him, but his passing left a void in the heart of his daughter, Meadow, who was just in her early teens.

Now, 22, Meadow has found love and a broad smile has returned to her face. Recently, the model has been open about her love life and recently shared a big announcement with her fans.

Paul Walker at the "Fast & Furious" press conference on March 13, 2009. | Photo: Getty Images

Earlier this month, Meadow announced her engagement to actor Louis Thornton-Allan, a month after she confirmed their relationship on social media. Since then, Walker’s little girl has had no fear of flaunting her man.

Recently, Meadow showed her sweet and loved-up side in a series of pictures she shared on her Instagram page, where she wished her man a happy birthday in style.

From the slides she shared, it was obvious Meadow was proud of her fiance. One of the pictures she shared showed the couple enjoying a loved-up moment with their facemask on.

Since her announcement of their relationship and engagement, fans have been curious to know more about their relationship and the background of the lucky man who swept Walker’s kid off her feet.

Keeping Walker's legacy is a dream she hopes to achieve.

Meadow announced her engagement with a teaser Instagram video that showed her sweetly showing off a diamond ring on her left hand while playing around in a pool.

Their romance seems to have been a fast one, but from Meadow’s post, their love seems to be genuine as she referred to the actor as her best friend in one of her posts.

Meadow’s fiance is yet to make his mark in Hollywood, but he is reportedly studying at the Stella Adler Studio of Acting in New York City, after which he hopes to become the next big thing.

The exact timeline of their relationship is unclear since Meadow tends to keep her private life away from the spotlight, but fans can view their sweet posts on social media to understand their strong connection.

Meanwhile, 22-year-old Meadow continues to make her late pops proud with her career as a model. A few years since she began her career, Meadow already has the top guys calling for her.

In addition to her budding career, Meadow honored her father’s memory by founding “The Paul Walker Foundation” in his honor. Keeping Walker's legacy is a dream she hopes to achieve.
