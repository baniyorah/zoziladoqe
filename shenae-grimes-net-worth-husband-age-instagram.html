Shenae Grimes Wiki Biography

Shenae Grimes was born on 24 October 1989, in Ottawa, Canada, and is an actress, best known for being a part of the TV series “Beverly Hills, 90210” spin-off entitled “90210”, in which she played the role of Annie Wilson. She also had a recurring role in the series “Naturally, Sadie”. She’s been active in the entertainment industry since 2004, and all of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Shenae Grimes? As of early-2018, sources inform us of a net worth that is at $4 million, mostly earned through a successful career as an actress, partly earned through appearances in several films too throughout her career. As she continues working, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

Shenae Grimes Net Worth $4 million

Shenae attended Forest Hill Collegiate Institute, and interned at the Toronto’s Fashion Television as a part of her curriculum. She then completed her high school education at the private school City Academy.

Grimes found her first acting role in 2004, when she was cast as the lead character in “Degrassi: The Next Generation”, playing the role of Darcy Edwards. The series is set in the “Degrassi” universe, and is the fourth in the franchise which follows an ensemble cast of students. For her performance in the show, she won a Gemini Award for Best Performance in a Children’s or Youth Program or Series. She then left the series in 2008, as she was cast in the CW show “90210”; like the original show, the series focused on several wealthy students who are a part of an upscale community in Beverly Hills, California, staying with the series until 2013.

More opportunities started to appear for her which helped increase her net worth. She was a part of “Picture This”, and “True Confessions of a Hollywood Starlet” in which she appeared alongside singer JoJo. In 2009 Grimes appeared in the music video entitled “All You Did Was Save My Life” by Our Lady Peace. At this time she was also named as one of the world’s most beautiful people without makeup by People magazine.

In 2010, Shenae tried her hand at writing and directing a music video entitled “Myself and I”. She also collaborated with YouTube star Freddie Wong on a video, and made a cameo appearance in the film “Scream 4”. She then went on a hiatus from “90210” and started working as an intern for Teen Vogue. She then directed the music video for the song “Are You Happy Now?”

One of her latest projects is the film “Sugar”, which follows the story of a runaway girl living in the streets of Venice, Los Angeles. In 2017, she made a guest appearance in the series “iZombie” in which she played the role of Piper, so her net worth is still rising.

For her personal life, it is known that Grimes married musician and model Josh Beech in 2013 – they became engaged in 2012 after nine months of dating, and married in 2013. In 2011 she was in Japan when the country was hit by various tsunamis and earthquakes, actually visiting a temple during the time the earthquakes occurred which she described as a very nervous time.
