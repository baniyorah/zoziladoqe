Inside singer Shania Twain's personal life: The Canadian singer has been married twice!  

Known for her myriad of hitmakers, Shania Twain is a famed singer-songwriter. Throughout her decades-spanning career, Twain has garnered a trove of esteemed accolades, including a few Grammys, multiple ACM awards, and more, making her one of the most decorated female country singers.  

Born on August 28, 1965, in Windsor, Canada, Twain has not only blurred the line between country and pop, which garnered her title of Queen of Country Pop but has also branched her repertoire in other areas of showbiz. Also, an actress, Twain's credits include I Heart Huckabees, Trading Paint, and I Still Believe.
