Who is Aaron Marino?

Aaron Marino (Alpha M) is an American male fashion enthusiast, lifestyle blogger, and fitness expert. He is active on YouTube and his channel AlphaM has over 6.29 M subscribers.

Additionally, he also appeared in ‘Shark Tank’ Season 7 finale to pitch his idea which led to the birth of his new company – Pete and Pedro.

Aaron Marino: Early Life, Ethnicity, Education

Marino was born in the United States on May 19, 1976. He had two step-fathers growing up.

His mother gave him a fitness membership card at the age of 13 which he claims changed his life.

He is of American nationality and has Caucasian ethnicity.

Talking about his education, Marino attended West Virginia University.

Aaron Marino: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Aaron Marino initially worked at Bally’s. Soon, along with his business partner, he opened a nutrition store.

Later, he went on to co-own another nutrition store. Eventually, in 2006, he formed his own image consulting firm called Alpha M. Additionally, in the same year, he released his M. Apparel clothing line.

Furthermore, Marino is the founder of IAmAlphaM and the owner of alpha m. Image Consulting. His blog focuses specifically on men who are below average height.

Currently, he spends most of his time making videos about style, grooming, dating, and fitness.

In addition, Marino is also a professional speaker, image consultant business adviser, host of corporate seminars & workshops, and expert author.

Marino has not revealed his current salary. Additionally as of 2022, He has an estimated net worth of $4.6 million.
