Who is Giada De Laurentiis?

Giada De Laurentiis is an American Television personality, chef, and writer. She hosts an American Television Food Network show ‘Giada at Home.

Giada is the founder of GDL Foods, her catering business. She is also the guest co-host for the U.S.TV program Today.

Giada De Laurentiis: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born on August 22, 1970, in Rome, Italy. Giada Pamela De Laurentiis is her birth name. She was born to parents Veronica De Laurentiis and Alex De Benedetti. Veronica is an actress and her husband Alex is an actor-producer.

Her maternal grandfather Dino De Laurentiis is a film producer. She has a sister Eloisa, and brothers Igor and Dino Alexander.

Her sister Eloisa is a makeup artist and her brother Dino was a Hollywood film editor. Later in 2003, Dino died of melanoma.

She often spent her time at her grandfather’s restaurant, the DDL Food show during her childhood. Giada moved to Southern California with her siblings after her parent’s divorce.

Giada De Laurentiis has dual nationality, American and Italian. She holds an Italian ethnicity.

Further, there are no details regarding her childhood.

In which school did Giada De Laurentiis attend?

Giada completed her high school education at Marymount High School in Los Angeles.

After high school, she enrolled at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Giada De Laurentiis graduated with a bachelor’s degree in social anthropology from the university in 1996.

Giada De Laurentiis: Professional Life, Career

Giada De Laurentiis studied at Le Cordon Bleu for becoming a pastry chef.

She started her career as a professional chef. she worked for several restaurants in Los Angeles.

Later she worked as a food stylist. In 2012, as a food stylist, she wrote an article about her family’s Sunday meal traditions for Food & Wine Magazine.

Giada De Laurentiis hosted ‘Everyday Italian’, a half-hour cooking show in 2003.

In October 2006, she hosted the show ‘Behind the Bash‘. In January 2007, Giada debuted a new show ‘Giada’s Weekend Gateways’.

She made appearances as a guest judge on ‘Next Food Network Star’.

In 2008 Giada De Laurentiis premiered the Television show ‘Giada at Home.

In 2009, she voiced the character of “Paulette” for the animated children’s show ‘Handy Manny’.Giada opened her restaurant, GIADA in July 2014.

The restaurant was situated inside ‘The Cromwell’ in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Giada De Laurentiis voiced the character of Gelata for ‘Pixie Hollow Bake Off’, the US version of the Tinker Bell Special in 2014.

Adding up more info she starred in a new Hyundai Motor Company Tv commercial Advert to promote the Hyundai Tucson.

Achievements and Awards

In 2008, Giada won the ‘Daytime Emmy Awards as an Outstanding Lifestyle Host for her work on Everyday Italian.

Similarly, she was nominated for ‘Daytime Emmy Awards in 2010 for her work on ‘Giada at Home. Giada won ‘Gracie Awards’ in 2012 for Giada at Home.

Recently, Giada De Laurentiis won the title of Daytime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Culinary Series.
