Who is Guy Fieri?

Guy Fieri is a restaurateur, author, game show host, and television personality who hails from America.

He gained a lot of popularity and fame as the host of the popular television series on the Food Network.

Guy Fieri: Career, Profession

Guy Fieri started his career by opening a restaurant partnering with Steve Gruber in 1996 in California.

They also opened restaurants in two more locations. Their restaurant is a combination of Southern BBQ with California-style sushi.
