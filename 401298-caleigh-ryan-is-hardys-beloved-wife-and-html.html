Caleigh Ryan Is Hardy's Beloved Wife and His Biggest Critic

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  623 words  |  Patria Henriques

Hardy's wife, Caleigh Ryan, inspired some of his songs and has played a crucial role in the singer's career. She keeps it real and never stops pointing out mistakes in Hardy's music.

Hardy is a talented singer-songwriter who rose to fame in 2020 after releasing his debut album "A Rock." He is also the brains behind hits songs such as "God's Country" and "Up and Down," among others.

When Hardy was a little kid, he pictured himself becoming a singer, but he began taking music seriously as a teenager.

Hardy's musical journey has seen him work with renowned country singers like Morgan Wallen, Lauren Alina, Blake Shelton, and more. When Hardy is not in the studio, he likes to spend quality time with his wife, Caleigh Ryan, who has helped him hone his song writing skills over the years.

Hardy Recently Got Married for the First Time to His Longtime Girlfriend Caleigh Ryan

Hardy and Ryan share an incredible love story. The pair started going out in April 2018, and after three years of dating, they got engaged in 2021. Hardy's proposal was one of a kind—he planned it at the same venue they met for the first time.

While it's been five months since Hardy and Ryan wedded, the singer is ready to become a father.

In an interview, Hardy revealed how he faked a private gig at The Lyric Theater to get Ryan to the venue without her suspecting anything. His plan worked out, and Ryan said yes to his proposal.

The singer took to his Instagram to celebrate their engagement with a photo of himself on one knee, asking Ryan to marry him. A few months before the proposal, Hardy met Ryan's father and asked his permission to marry his daughter.

Hardy and Caleigh Ryan walked down the aisle in Nashville on October 29, 2022. Their wedding was held at Diamond Creek's Farm, and Ryan revealed they chose the venue because it was brand new and stunning.

For the big day, the bride stole the spotlight with her custom-designed white gown after failing to find a wedding dress she liked at a bridal shop. Hardy's and Ryan's first dance as husband and wife was to the song "Faithfully" by Journey.

Caleigh Ryan Is Hardy's Biggest Supporter and Critic

While Hardy is a successful singer, Ryan also has an accomplished career spanning different fields. Her LinkedIn profile shows she works as a Marketing Coordinator at Graduate Hotels. Aside from that, Hardy's wife is also a social media influencer with at least 168K followers as of this writing.

Ryan and Hardy have a great relationship. While Ryan has supported Hardy's music career throughout the years, the singer revealed that his wife is his biggest critic. During an interview with PEOPLE, Hardy said:

"We've been together for over two years, so now she's my biggest critic. She's not going to hold back, and she's going to tell me if she loves it."

Ryan has inspired many of Hardy's songs, including his hit song "Boyfriend." Speaking to PEOPLE, Hardy revealed how the song came out. He narrated how he and Ryan fought on vacation while still dating.

During the heated argument, he said he no longer wanted to be Ryan's boyfriend, and to his surprise, Ryan responded that she wanted him to be her husband. Ryan's cute reaction inspired Hardy, and at that moment, he thought of composing the song "Boyfriend."

Hardy and Caleigh Ryan Are Already Thinking about Kids

While it's been five months since Hardy and Ryan wedded, the singer is ready to become a father. In an interview with Taste of Country, Hardy revealed he was prepared to have kids and hoped to share his passion for music with his future children.
