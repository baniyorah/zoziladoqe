Who is Lisa Vidal?

Lisa Vidal is an American actress, singer, and producer. She rose to fame for her role in the Lifetime crime drama series The Division and BET drama series Being Mary Jane.

What is the birth date of Lisa Vidal?

Lisa was born to Josie Vidal (mother) and Manny Vidal (father). Her birth date is on June 13, 1965, and raised in Whitestone, Queens, New York. Her father was a tax consultant and businessman whereas her mother was a secretary by profession.

She has two younger sisters named Christina Vidal and Tanya Vidal and a brother named Christian Vidal.

Her both sisters are also well-known actresses. Talking about her academic background, she studied at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School.

After graduation, she worked in La Familia Theater Company, alongside Raul Julia and Julia Roberts.

Her nationality is American and belongs to Puerto Rican descent.

Lisa Vidal: Profession Life, Career

Lisa made her acting debut through the theatre series Oye Willie at the age of 14. In 1985, she was featured in the 1985 Ken Handler film Delivery Boys. Following this, she starred in a guest role in various television series such as Christmas Eve (1986), The Cosby Show (1987), ABC Afterschool Special (1987, 1991), Miami Vice (1988), and more.

She has major recurring roles in the ABC police drama High Incident from 1995 to 1996 and on The Brian Benben Show in 1998. She portrayed the role of Dr. Sarah Morales on the NBC crime drama television series, the Third Watch from 1999 to 2001. Similarly, some of her notable films are The Third Twin (1997), The Taking of Pelham One Two Three (1998), Odd Girl Out (2005), Dark Mirror (2007), and more.

Recently, she starred on the ABC medical drama television series, Grey’s Anatomy as Dr. Alma Ortiz.
