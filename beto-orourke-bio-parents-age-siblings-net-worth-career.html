The bio of Beto O’Rourke covers details about his parents, age, siblings, net worth, and career. Robert Francis “Beto” O’Rourke is a multi-talented figure from the United States, excelling as a musician, writer, politician, and businessman.

Biography & Profile Summary

Beto O’Rourke was born on 26 September 1972 in El Paso, Texas, and is currently 50 years old. He holds American nationality and follows the Christian-Catholic faith. O’Rourke received education from Escuela Montessori Del Valle, Rivera Primary School, and Mestia Elementary School.

Beto O’Rourke Parents, Siblings & Family

Beto O’Rourke’s parents were Pat and Melissa Martha O’Rourke. His father, Pat O’Rourke, served as a County Commissioner and passed away in 2001. His mother, Melissa Martha O’Rourke, was a homemaker. Beto has a sister, Charlotte O’Rourke, and other half-siblings.

Beto O’Rourke Career

O’Rourke represented Texas’ 16th congressional district from 2013 to 2019. He ran for the presidency in 2020 and aspired to be Texas’ governor in 2022. O’Rourke’s political journey took shape in El Paso, Texas, and he pursued education at Woodberry Forest School and Columbia University. At Columbia, he briefly pursued music as the bassist for the band Foss.

In 2005, O’Rourke ventured into business and subsequently served on the El Paso City Council until 2011. His political ambition led him to contest against Ted Cruz for the US Senate seat, achieving substantial voter support. He later aimed for the presidency but halted his campaign in 2019.

Beto O’Rourke Wife

O’Rourke is married to Amy Hoover Sanders, and they have been together since 2005.

Beto O’Rourke Children

O’Rourke and Amy are parents to three children: Henry, Molly, and Ulysses O’Rourke.
