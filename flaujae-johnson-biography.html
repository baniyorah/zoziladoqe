When Flau’jae was very young, she began to sing. She was allowed to perform live as the daughter of a well-known rapper.

Guns Down

In her Judge Cuts performance, Flau’jae raps a rendition of her original song, “I Can’t Lose”. Howie Mandel, Mel B, Heidi Klum, Simon Cowell and guest judge Chris Hardwick gave her standing ovations.

Chris hit the Golden Buzzer after seeing Flau’jae play well, advancing her straight to the Quarterfinals. During her Week 1 Quarterfinals performance, Flau’jae performed her original song “Letdown” by rapping.

She received standing ovations from Simon Cowell, Heidi Klum, and Howie Mandel.

She was eliminated from the competition along with The PAC Dance Team rather than We Three because she did not collect enough votes to move on to the Semifinals.
